# Englozi

## Learn the Lozi Language with Ease...

Englozi is a language-learning tool (with over 20000 words) designed to help users master the Lozi language, whether they're complete beginners or advanced learners. With a comprehensive database of words, from basic to advanced, Englozi makes learning Lozi accessible and efficient.


Features
1. Bilingual Dictionary - Search for English words to get their Lozi translations, complete with multiple definitions.
2. Enhanced Learning - Discover synonyms, antonyms, and usage examples for better comprehension.
3. Flexible Use - Ideal for non-speakers, learners, and even native Lozi speakers looking to deepen their understanding.
4. Quick Search - Simply type an English word to instantly see its Lozi equivalents and related terms.


Who Can Use Englozi?
1. Beginners - Start learning Lozi from scratch with basic vocabulary.
2. Students - Enhance your studies with detailed definitions and contextual examples.
3. Advanced Learners - Expand your vocabulary with nuanced words and phrases.
4. Native Speakers - Refine your knowledge with synonyms, antonyms, and varied meanings.

How It Works
1. Search - Enter an English word in the search bar.
2. Explore - View Lozi translations, multiple definitions, and related terms.
3. Learn: - Study examples, synonyms, and antonyms to grasp word usage.


Englozi simplifies Lozi language learning—making it interactive, practical, and adaptable to all levels.
